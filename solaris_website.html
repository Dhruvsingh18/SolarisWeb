01 — The Problem

Indonesia's cold chain is broken.

Across 17,000 islands and 270 million people, the archipelago moves billions in perishable cargo every day—on ships without reliable cooling. The cost is enormous and preventable.

01 / Spoilage
20% of all cargo is lost
23–27 million tonnes of perishables wasted annually. Consumers pay 400–500% above market rate because the cold chain fails island to island.
$2.8B annual loss to industry
02 / Fuel
Diesel refrigeration is hemorrhaging cash
Refrigerated units consume 15–20% more fuel. Operational costs surged 25–30% over three years. IMO 2025 mandates are forcing a reckoning now.
IMO 2025 mandates forcing change
03 / Reliability
Power outages sink entire shipments
Smaller Indonesian islands face chronic grid instability. One failure mid-voyage can destroy hundreds of thousands in cargo—with no recovery.
Average incident cost: $140K+

02 — The Product

One unit.
Total cold chain control.

Mounts on any vessel in 48 hours. No dry-docking. No downtime. Generates its own power, stores it, and uses AI to keep cargo perfect regardless of what the grid does.

☀
12 kW Solar Array
Monocrystalline silicon with nano-coating. AI-optimized load balancing maximizes every hour of sunlight across the voyage.
⚡
48 kWh LiFePO₄ Battery
Six cell stacks in redundant config. 4.2 hours full autonomy through cloud cover, night voyages, and grid failure.
🧠
LSTM + Transformer AI Brain
18 sensor streams simultaneously. Detects cargo type, predicts temperature drift 6 hours ahead. Responds in under 30ms. Learns fleet-wide.
❄
R290 Cryo Cooling
Zero ozone depletion. -25°C cargo hold, +5°C crew zone simultaneously. Variable-speed compressor cuts energy draw up to 40%.
